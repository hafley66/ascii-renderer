## Key Insights
- Boles are root flares, not architecture. Most multi-layer styles (Frame, Frame2, Frame3, Chevron stacks) grow 5-25 rows and look like standalone bushes. Hard cap all to 2-3 rows.
- The worktree agent pattern works for research but fails for writes due to sandbox permissions. Better approach: use agents for planning, apply changes directly.
- forest4 has visual personality types (diagonal trees, explody tendril trees, face-looking tree, kaiju) that are missing from the TreeDrawer system. These are the "vibes" gap.
- PineTree/WillowTree/PalmTree are visually distinct enough from existing 8 TreeDrawer types to justify dedicated impls rather than recipe parametrization.
