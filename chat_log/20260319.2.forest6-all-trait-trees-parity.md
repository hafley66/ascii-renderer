## Key Insights
- grow() should not impose branch-attachment structure. Each tree's draw_branch owns everything from the junction outward. Discovered when stray ├─ appeared at trunk tops for crown-type trees.
- Crown trees (candelabra, split) use should_branch firing once at trunk top, with draw_branch handling the entire crown. No grow() override needed.
- Floating diagonals (╱╲) don't connect with box-drawing verticals. Corner pairs (╰╮ for lean-left, ╭╯ for lean-right) produce clean connections.
- Fruit placement needs a separate pass after structure is complete, not inline during growth.
- Each tree type benefits from a distinct trunk style for visual identity at a glance.
